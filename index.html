Logitech from the inside: how to create and test gaming mice By Henri Sergeev, December 19, 2014, 00:00 Logitech does not just test mice and keyboards, they are simply "tortured" here. The keys are pressed 13 times a second, 24 hours a day, for two months. When Logitech engineers developed the G402 Hyperion Fury gaming mouse, which accurately tracks the cursor movement at a speed of more than 450 inches/sec, a mechanical arm with a built-in spring was designed, which was supposed to test the mouse in the harshest conditions until the latter failed.
